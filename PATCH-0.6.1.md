# We Ride at Dawn — v0.6.1

- 🐀 **New: the Bench.** Keep rats out of the fighting horde — stockpile copies until you've got three to merge, and hold counter-rats to swap in against the day's foes. Buy past a full board and the overflow waits on the bench.
- 🔁 **Swap in and out** straight from a rat's card — no more selling just to move a bench rat onto a full board.
- ⚖️ **Merge refund.** Fold three rats that share a trinket and you get **half its scrap back**, instead of losing the spare for nothing.
- ⚔️ The warren now **tells you when a fresh build has ridden in** — tap to reload, so you're never stuck on a stale version.

_No changes to the gauntlet or the leaderboard curve — your week rides on._
